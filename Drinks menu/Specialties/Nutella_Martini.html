The Decadent Nutella Martini

A dessert lover's dream, the Nutella Martini combines the beloved hazelnut chocolate spread with premium spirits for an indulgent treat. This modern creation is perfect for those who want their dessert in a glass.

Origin: The Nutella Martini is a contemporary dessert cocktail that gained popularity in the 2000s as bartenders began experimenting with popular food flavors in drinks. Inspired by the iconic Italian spread, it quickly became a favorite at upscale bars.

Why It's Special: This cocktail transforms the classic childhood flavor of Nutella into a sophisticated adult beverage. Rich, creamy, and utterly satisfying, it's the perfect ending to any meal or a treat on its own.


INGREDIENTS
Main Ingredients:
  • 2 oz Vanilla Vodka
  • 1 oz Frangelico (hazelnut liqueur)
  • 1 oz Chocolate Liqueur
  • 2 oz Heavy Cream
  • 1 tbsp Nutella

Garnishes:
  • Nutella rim
  • Chocolate shavings
  • Hazelnut pieces
  • Whipped cream (optional)
BASE ALCOHOL MODIFICATIONS

White Chocolate Replace with White Chocolate Liqueur
Lighter, sweeter variation
Peanut Butter Cup Add Peanut Butter Whiskey
Classic candy combination
Baileys Nutella Add Baileys Irish Cream
