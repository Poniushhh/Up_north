The Refreshing Strawberry Daiquiri Slushie

Sweet, tart, and frozen to perfection, our Strawberry Daiquiri Slushie is a crowd favorite. Fresh strawberries blended with rum and lime create a drink that's both refreshing and delicious.

Origin: The classic Daiquiri was invented in Cuba in the early 1900s. The frozen strawberry version became wildly popular in the 1970s and 80s, bringing a fruity twist to the Cuban classic.

Why It's Special: This frozen delight perfectly balances the sweetness of strawberries with the tartness of fresh lime. The slushie texture makes it incredibly refreshing and dangerously easy to drink.


INGREDIENTS
Main Ingredients:
  • 2 oz White Rum
  • 1 cup Fresh Strawberries
  • 1 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1 oz Simple Syrup
  • 2 cups Ice

Garnishes:
  • Fresh strawberry slice
  • Lime wheel
  • Mint sprig (optional)
