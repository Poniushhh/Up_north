The Tropical Piña Colada Slushie

Our frozen version of the classic Piña Colada brings Caribbean vibes straight to your glass. Creamy coconut, sweet pineapple, and smooth rum blended into icy perfection—it's vacation in a cup.

Origin: The original Piña Colada was created in Puerto Rico in the 1950s and became the island's national drink. Our slushie version adds a frosty twist to this beloved tropical classic.

Why It's Special: This frozen delight is incredibly refreshing and transports you to a tropical paradise with every sip. The slushie texture makes it even more fun and perfect for hot days.


INGREDIENTS
Main Ingredients:
  • 2 oz White Rum
  • 3 oz Pineapple Juice
  • 2 oz Coconut Cream
  • 2 cups Ice
  • ½ oz Simple Syrup (optional)

Garnishes:
  • Fresh pineapple wedge
  • Maraschino cherry
  • Paper umbrella
