The Party-Ready Fish Bowl

The Fish Bowl is a fun, shareable cocktail served in a large bowl, perfect for groups. With its bright blue color and playful gummy fish garnish, it's as Instagram-worthy as it is delicious.

Origin: This novelty cocktail became popular in the 1990s at beach bars and party venues. The oversized presentation and tropical flavors made it an instant hit for celebrations and group gatherings.

Why It's Special: The Fish Bowl isn't just a drink—it's an experience. Served with multiple straws for sharing, this tropical concoction brings people together and creates memorable moments.


INGREDIENTS
Main Ingredients:
  • 2 oz Blue Curaçao
  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Coconut Rum
  • 4 oz Pineapple Juice
  • 2 oz Sweet & Sour Mix
  • Splash of Lemon-Lime Soda

Garnishes:
  • Gummy Fish
  • Orange slices
  • Straws for sharing
BASE ALCOHOL MODIFICATIONS
Red Fish Bowl Use Grenadine & Cherry Vodka
Cranberry-red color variation
Green Fish Bowl Use Melon Liqueur instead
Bright green tropical twist
Purple Fish Bowl Add Chambord & Grape Juice
Berry-flavored purple version
Tequila Fish Bowl Replace vodka with Tequila
Mexican beach party style
