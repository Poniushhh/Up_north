The Energizing Espresso Martini

The Espresso Martini is the perfect pick-me-up cocktail, combining the richness of coffee with smooth vodka. This modern classic is elegant, caffeinated, and utterly delicious with its signature foam top.

Origin: Created in 1983 by legendary bartender Dick Bradsell at London's Soho Brasserie. The story goes that a supermodel asked for a drink that would "wake me up and then mess me up." The result became one of the most popular cocktails of the modern era.

Why It's Special: This cocktail is both a stimulant and a relaxant, making it perfect for late-night gatherings. The beautiful crema foam on top and the bold coffee flavor make it a sophisticated choice for coffee lovers.


INGREDIENTS
Main Ingredients:
  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Coffee Liqueur (Kahlúa)
  • 1 oz Fresh Espresso (cooled)
  • ½ oz Simple Syrup

Garnishes:
  • 3 Coffee beans
  • Espresso foam top
BASE ALCOHOL MODIFICATIONS

Smooth whiskey meets coffee
Vanilla Espresso Use Vanilla Vodka
Sweet vanilla enhances coffee

Creamy and indulgent version
