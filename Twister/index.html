TWISTERS ICE CREAM

Eagle Bay's Favorite Ice Cream Shop Since 1985


Welcome to Twisters!

Located directly across the street from Up North Wood Fire & Grill Bar, Twisters Ice Cream has been serving the Eagle Bay community and visitors for nearly 40 years. Our family-owned shop specializes in homemade ice cream, frozen yogurt, and classic American ice cream treats.

Every batch of ice cream is made fresh on-site using traditional recipes and the finest ingredients. From classic vanilla to adventurous flavors like Adirondack Maple Walnut and Blueberry Cheesecake, we have something for everyone!

Open seasonally from May through September, Twisters is the perfect way to end your meal at Up North or to cool down on a hot summer day in the Adirondacks.
